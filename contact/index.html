— Frequently asked

Before you ask.

  • Do you work outside Cascais?

    Yes — anywhere within a 30–40 minute drive of the Cascais studio. That covers Estoril, Sintra, Carcavelos, Parede, Alcabideche, and into Lisbon for the right brief.

  • What languages does Maja speak?

    Seven: English, Portuguese, Italian, French, Spanish, German, and Slovenian.

  • Do you handle permits?

    Yes. IMPIC, AMI, municipal heritage permits in Cascais and Sintra, and condominium liaison — all handled in-house.

  • What’s the smallest project you take on?

    A single-room renovation, typically a kitchen or a bathroom. Below that, we’ll usually point you toward a trade rather than a full studio engagement.

  • Do you work with clients buying property from abroad?

    Often. We can find the property, manage the purchase, and renovate to handover. See Services.

  • What does a typical renovation cost?

    Highly project-specific. Bathrooms typically €25k–60k, kitchens €40k–120k, full apartments €150k–500k. Final pricing reflects materials and complexity.
