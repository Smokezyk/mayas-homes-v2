— Philosophy

Five principles.

One designer, start to finish.

The same person who draws the first sketch hands you the keys. No translation losses, no hand-offs that lose intent.

Zero subcontractors.

Every trade is on our payroll. The person laying the tile knows the person who designed the room.

Specified, then built.

Every wall, every fitting, every finish is drawn, costed, and signed off before a tool is lifted. No surprise invoices.

Built for year ten.

Materials and details chosen for how they age. Salt-air joinery, breathable plasters, fittings sourced for longevity, not list price.

We never really leave.

A bulb in year three is still our problem. Maja answers personally.
