— The signature commission

A home held by one hand, beginning to end.

A full renovation at Maya’s Homes begins with a site walk and ends, six to ten months later, with a key on a polished countertop. In between: drawings, demolition, ten trades, hundreds of decisions — every one of them taken by the same person who first imagined the room. No subcontracted floor crew. No outside design studio handing the file to a builder it has never met. One studio, one schedule, one hand.

Every full renovation includes complete architectural drawings, full demolition and structural rebuild where required, all trades in-house (joinery, electrical, plumbing, tiling, masonry, drywall, finishing), every finish and fixture sourced and installed by the studio, weekly client updates, and a year of aftercare. Optional: turnkey delivery (Service № 05) for clients who want to walk into a finished home rather than a finished shell.

A typical project runs six to ten months. Scope, season, and listing constraints decide where in that range yours lands. We will tell you honestly, before you sign.

— Before contracts

A second pair of eyes before you sign.

Most renovation regret begins on the day a property is bought, not the day it is renovated. The wrong wall, the wrong orientation, the wrong neighbour, a foundation problem hidden behind fresh paint. Maja walks the property with you — before contracts, not after — and tells you, plainly, what the renovation will actually cost and whether the home is worth the work.

A single morning. A written summary within the week. No commission, no agency relationship, no incentive to say yes.

— A short note on what we don’t do

The studio does not take cosmetic-only commissions, single-room paint refreshes, or rush jobs under three months. Every home we build is held to the same standard, and that standard takes time. If your project is small or fast, we are happy to recommend someone we trust.

— A note on pricing

Costs are quoted privately, after a site walk. We do not publish a price list — every home is different, and a quote made before seeing the room is a quote made wrong. Most clients receive a written estimate within ten days of their first meeting with Maja.
