— What we offer

The design is yours. The standard is ours.

Every renovation is shaped by the client. The build is shaped by us — one team, one set of materials, one standard. Six services, all in-house.

  • Full home renovations

    Apartments, lofts, villas, restaurant-to-residence conversions. Every wall, every fitting, every finish drawn first, costed second, built third.

    Permits handled in-house. Demolition through final inspection by the same team. Drawings revised in person, on site, in your language.

    Typical brief: 80–300 m², 12–24 weeks.

    What’s included

    • Walk-through & brief
    • Drawings + 3D renders
    • Materials specification
    • Permits (IMPIC, AMI, municipal)
    • Demolition through finishes
    • Final inspection & snag-list clearing

    Example: Monte Estoril Apartment →

  • Kitchens & bathrooms

    The two rooms that decide whether a home feels considered. Custom joinery, hand-laid stone, fittings selected for salt-air longevity.

    Often commissioned alone — often the start of a fuller renovation a year or two later.

    See the dedicated K&B page for approach, materials, and pricing range.

    What’s included

    • Custom joinery design
    • Stone selection at the yard
    • Hardware & fittings spec
    • Plumbing & electrical rework
    • Tiling & finishing

    Read more →

  • Structural & permits

    Walls moved. Beams added. Engineering signed and stamped.

    IMPIC, AMI, Cascais and Sintra preservation permits handled in-house, in your language. Seven years means we know what each town hall expects.

    What’s included

    • Structural engineering
    • IMPIC + AMI submissions
    • Heritage permits where applicable
    • Condominium liaison
    • As-built documentation

  • Property finding & turnkey relocation

    For clients buying in Cascais from abroad. We find the property, manage the purchase, then renovate, furnish, and inspect — so the keys we hand you open a home that’s already ready to live in.

    Most clients arrive with no Portuguese. Maja speaks seven languages.

    What’s included

    • Brief + neighborhood orientation
    • Off-market & on-market search
    • Lawyer & notary coordination
    • Full renovation
    • Furniture, dressing, lighting
    • Inspection before handover

  • Pet-friendly homes

    Homes designed with the whole household in mind, including the pets. Durable finishes, integrated nooks, materials that won’t show daily wear — all woven into the design, not added on.

    What’s included

    • Pet-considered floor specification
    • Integrated feeding & sleeping areas
    • Outdoor washing zones
    • Material durability tested

  • Innovative materials & imports

    Sourcing beyond the local market when the project calls for it. Emerging finishes, imports of high-quality materials from abroad, technologies not yet widely available in Portugal — all vetted through our own trades before any project sees them.

    What’s included

    • European & international sourcing
    • Sample acquisition & review
    • Customs & logistics handled
    • Application by our trades
